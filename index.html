    Calming Tools

    🌬

    Extended Exhale Breathing

    5-10 min

    4-count inhale, 1-2 hold, 6-8 exhale. Activates your parasympathetic nervous system.

    🌿

    5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

    60 sec

    Redirect attention to sensory input. Interrupts the amygdala cascade in real time.

    💪

    Progressive Muscle Relaxation

    15 min

    Tense and release 11 muscle groups. Lowers baseline arousal so triggers produce smaller spikes.

    ⏸

    Time-Out Protocol

    8-10 min

    5-step structured time-out: Notice, Signal, Move, Regulate, Return.

    🦶

    Feet on the Floor Reset

    30 sec

    Press feet into the ground, breathe out slowly. Instant physical grounding before responding.

    Cognitive Tools

    🧠

    CBT Thought Record

    15-20 min

    6-column thought record. Identify distortions and reframe automatic thoughts.

    🔄

    DBT Opposite Action

    5 min

    Act opposite to the anger urge. Reduces the emotion's intensity and duration.

    ☁️

    ACT Defusion

    10 min

    Create psychological distance from angry thoughts. Observe them rather than be dominated.

    🗣

    DEAR MAN Communication

    15 min

    Express needs assertively without attacking or withdrawing. Script difficult conversations.

    💛

    Self-Compassion

    3 min

    After an episode you're disappointed with. Not self-indulgence — a neurological calming act.
